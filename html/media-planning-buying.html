Media Planning & Buying
Effective Media Planning & Buying

Google and several other online advertising platforms have enabled companies to buy spaces for their promotions. With such an easy access to advertisement spaces, companies often wonder why their cash isn’t resulting into real buyers?

Focusing on Media Buying without effective planning only drains a company’s wallet. No matter what size your company is, you need to go through effective Media Planning process before you can move ahead with where you will be promoting your brand.

While buying agencies find the best distribution channels for any ad campaign, you cannot get maximum advantage of those hard-to-get slots if your campaign itself isn’t creative and suitable for that distribution channel. A traditional billboard requires a different kind of campaign than one on Facebook that could take advantage of Motion Graphics.

Media Planning and Media Buying

At Boardroom, Media Planning and Media Buying work hand in hand. This means our creative directors are always in sync with the distribution experts to make sure our clients get the most out of their money and efforts.

Strategic Media Planing

Once we develop a comprehensive and strategic media plan, we work on a creative campaign or advertisement. Only after that does the agency begin the buying process. This process also doesn’t mean going to whatever channel sounds fancy, in vogue or easy to get – it means we secure the best position at the best time according to the campaign and the brand we are promoting.
