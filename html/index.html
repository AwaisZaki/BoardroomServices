Who we are
Boardroom.Media is a leading digital multimedia production company specialising in quality audio and video production and webcasting services for listed companies, asset managers and professional services firms.

At boardroom.media we live to tell stories that connect audiences with the ideas that are shaping tomorrow. Our team of experienced multimedia producers and storytellers offer a new angle on the issues of the day, and share insights from trusted commentators across diverse sectors, including financial markets, technology, professional services and lifestyle. We're constantly on the look out for new stories and new ways to tell them, so if you have a tip off, we'd love to hear from you.
