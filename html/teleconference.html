Teleconferencing
Straight from the source

At Boardroom.Media, we understands the critical importance of your market communications and offer a complete range of Investor Relations services that will help you reach stakeholders in remote locations and extend the reach of your message.

Our reliable, secure and easy to use technology incorporates robust host controls that allow you to schedule and manage your financial results, important ASX announcements, quarterly results, press releases and employee updates.

All teleconference and streaming solutions are fully managed including the booking process; speaker and participant dial-in numbers; moderated question and answer sessions; live and on-demand conference call streaming, audio post-production and data capture/reporting so you can focus on the message, not the method of delivery.

Our premium Investor Teleconferencing service includes:
 
  • Test event will be scheduled and conducted by administrator 24 hrs prior to your event
  • Real time chat with the operator via IM or backline
 
  • Pre event sub-conferencing to allow presenters to prepare.
  • Equipment and sound quality testing
 
  • Global access numbers, local and free call
  • Guaranteed ratio of 1 operator to each 15 participants expected
 
  • Multiple carrier numbers provided to ensure a carrier outage will not affect your call
  • Tier 1 technology supported by redundant power and systems
 
  • Operator managed Q&A session with web based tools to view participants and control the question queue
  • Post Call – participant list and recording of call available within 30 minutes
 
  • Digital playback – for a nominated period of time the conference will be available for a replay by dialling into our system
